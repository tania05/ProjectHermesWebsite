Your privacy matters
Keep your messages secret with Project Hermes

Start Sending Private Messages

Maintaining your privacy is our top priority! With Project Hermes, you will send encrypted messages across a peer-to-peer network without transmitting meta data — governments and corporations won’t be able to track your messages. As an added bonus, everyone who helps carry messages across the network will have the potential to earn money through the power of Ethereum.

Private

Project Hermes does not send meta data, and all messages are encrypted to maintain your privacy.

Peer to Peer

Your messages do not travel across the Internet. Instead they travel across a peer-to-peer mesh network.

Make Money

Users who help carry messages across the network have the potential to earn money.

Android

Project Hermes is available on Android devices.

Open Source

The source code for Project Hermes is available on GitLab.

Add Contacts

Scan the QR codes of users whom you’d like to message. This will automatically save these users as contacts.

Send a Message

Select one of your contacts and type a message. When ready, tap send. This will encrypt the message and distribute it into the peer-to-peer network. Sending a message requires you to pay a small amount of money, which will be sent to a smart contract that handles the distribution of money to message carriers.

Wait for Message to Travel

The message propagates across the peer-to-peer network until it reaches the intended recipient. Each user that helps carry the message is automatically added to the smart contract.

Receive a Message

The recipient of the message is notified of the incoming message and is able to read it. The smart contract is now free to pick a random user that helped carry the message and send the money to that user.
