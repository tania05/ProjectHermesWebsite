An Overview of Project Hermes

Project Hermes is a peer-to-peer messaging application that runs on Android. Maintaining your privacy is our top priority: we use military grade encryption on all messages, and your messages are never distributed across the Internet. Because your messages travel across a peer-to-peer network as opposed to the Internet, we can eliminate meta data associated with your messages to further protect your privacy. Project Hermes also utilizes Ethereum to allow message carriers to make money while distributing messages across the peer-to-peer network.

Private

Peer to Peer
