Your privacy matters
Keep your messages secret with Project Hermes

Start Sending Private Messages

Maintaining your privacy is our top priority! With Project Hermes, you will send encrypted messages across a peer-to-peer network without transmitting meta data — governments and corporations won’t be able to track your messages. As an added bonus, everyone who helps carry messages across the network will have the potential to earn money through the power of Ethereum.

Download Project Hermes

Source Code

Project Hermes is open source. We want you to feel confident that your messages are as secure as possible!

View source code on GitLab

Private

Project Hermes does not send meta data, and all messages are encrypted to maintain your privacy.

Peer to Peer

Your messages do not travel across the Internet. Instead they travel across a peer-to-peer mesh network.

Make Money

Users who help carry messages across the network have the potential to earn money.

Android

Project Hermes is available on Android devices.

Open Source

The source code for Project Hermes is available on GitLab.

The Team

Rahat Mahbub

Team Lead & Software Architect

Rahat has been responsible for providing the overall design of Project Hermes. His work includes developing a message exchange protocol to ensure that messages will reach their destinations. He has also had a key role in integrating the Android application with the Ethereum smart contract.

Tania Akter

Frontend Engineer

Tania has been responsible for the design and implementation of the Android application's user interface. She has also had a role in the overall development of the Android application and messaging between devices.

Liam Scholte

Security Engineer

Liam has been responsible for developing the encryption protocols so that messages remain secure. He has also focused on the development of the Ethereum smart contract and how it interfaces with other applications.

Brandon Mabey

Network Engineer

Brandon has been responsible for implementing peer-to-peer networking on Android devices. He has also had a role in the overall development of the Android application and integration with Ethereum.
